About us

While education is increasingly becoming a corporate enterprise, we never think like a corporation. We are a group of students, entrepreneurs, creators, and medical professionals aiming to alter public perceptions of education and make it more accessible to everyone. We will constantly fight against becoming a conventional corporation since we have no desire to do so.
